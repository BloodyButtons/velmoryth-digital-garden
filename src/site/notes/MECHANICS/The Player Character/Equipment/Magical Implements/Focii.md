<center>MEANS OF UNBRIDLED POWER</center>

---

**Arcane Foci** serve as **spellcasting implements** for both dedicated mages and hybrid builds looking to augment their magical power without committing fully to a staff. Whether it’s a crystalline orb, a grim tome brimming with runic scrawl, a formidable staff, or a compact wand, these tools provide direct bonuses to **[[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Attributes/Intelligence\|Intelligence]]** (for damage output) or **[[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Attributes/Mind\|Mind]]** (for spell memorization). The most **rare and powerful** foci grant both bonuses, a boon for the most demanding arcs of arcane might.

---

## One-Handed vs. Two-Handed Foci

### One-Handed Foci

- **Common Forms**: Orbs, Wands, Grimoires, Charms  

- **Advantages**:  
  
  - Can be **dual-wielded** (e.g., wand + orb) for stacking INT/MIND bonuses.  
 
  - Frees up a hand for a small shield or light weapon in hybrid builds.  

- **Trade-offs**:  
  
  - Usually have **smaller base bonuses** to attributes than staves.  
 
  - Using two different foci adds synergy but drains more resources (e.g [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/General Stats/Mana\|Mana]])

### Two-Handed Foci (Staves)  

- **Common Forms**: Quarterstaff-like rods, Warstaves, Tall grimoires with attached rods.  

- **Advantages**:  
  
  - **Higher baseline** INT or MIND bonus.  
  
  - [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Skills and Talents/Talents/Talents\|Talents]] specialized in staves may boost area-of-effect spells or enhance destructive or [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Skills and Talents/Magic/Magical Skills/Conjuring/Conjuration\|Conjuration]] spells.  

- **Trade-offs**:  
  
  - Usually occupies both hands, limiting weapon usage or shield options. **Staves however can be one-handed for 50% of their effectiveness.** 
  - Typically heavier on [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/General Stats/Stamina\|stamina]] usage in [[MECHANICS/The Core Pillars/Combat\|combat]] if used for [[MECHANICS/Combat/Components/Parrying\|parrying]] or staff-based melee.

| Foci Type  | IL Range | Attribute Bonuses  | Notable Use Cases                                            |
|------------|---------|---------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Orb**    | 10–30    | +1–3 INT or +1–3 MIND (often single attribute) | Easy to dual-wield; can pair with dagger or shield for hybrids |
| **Wand**   | 10–35    | +1–3 INT or MIND, sometimes small ranged spells | Faster cast times; synergy with quick illusions or bursts     |
| **Grimoire**| 15–40   | +1–3 INT or MIND, can store extra spells        | Off-hand form of “memory extension,” great for Mind-based builds |
| **Staff**  | 20–50    | +2–5 INT or MIND, sometimes both at higher rarity | Two-handed, bigger base bonus, strong synergy with advanced arcane talents |

## Attributes & Buildcrafting

### Intelligence vs. Mind Focus

- **Intelligence (INT):**  
  
  - Increases raw spell damage (e.g., [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Skills and Talents/Magic/Magical Skills/Destruction/Destruction\|Destruction]] spells), reduces mana costs for certain arcane schools.  
 
  - Perfect for direct damage wizards or illusions with strong interplay in controlling fights.  

- **Mind (MIN):**  
 
  - Expands your ability to **memorize multiple spells** simultaneously, resists mental attacks, and improves advanced illusions/psionics.  
  
  - Suited for specialized conjurers or illusions-based builds that juggle numerous spells or require strong mental defenses.

#### Rare Dual-Bonus Foci

- Top-tier or legendary foci might grant **+INT and +MIND**, extremely sought after, as they eliminate the usual trade-off between raw damage and greater spell memory.  

- Often require high attribute thresholds (both INT and MIND) to prevent magical recoil or partial effect.

### Hybrid Approaches

- **Weapon + One-Handed Focus**: For Dex-based archers or Str-based knights dabbling in magic. You keep a spear, sword, or bow in the main hand and an orb/wand in the off-hand. This yields moderate INT or MIND bonuses without fully sacrificing physical combat.  

- **Dual Wield Foci**: Pure caster style: orb + grimoire, orb + wand, or wand + wand. This can accumulate small but stacking attribute bonuses for illusions or destructive spells.  

- **Staff**: The definitive two-handed caster’s route, maximizing arcane potency but limiting conventional weapon access.

## Combat & Survival Interactions

1. **Offensive Spellcasting**  
   
   - Foci specialized in Destruction, illusions, or conjuration intensify or shorten cast times, crucial in fast-paced skirmishes or boss fights.  
 
   - Switching foci mid-battle (where allowed) may shift from single-target to area-of-effect focus, though this can risk stamina or mental strain.

2. **Spell Memorization & Efficiency**  
   
   - MIND-boosting foci expand your repertoire, letting you keep illusions plus conjurations plus an emergency healing spell all “readied” simultaneously.  
   
   - Additional synergy if your race or talents revolve around psionics or illusions.

3. **[[MECHANICS/The Player Character/General Stats/Encumbrance\|Encumbrance]] & Stamina**  
   
   - Generally, foci are low-weight, except for heavier staves that demand moderate Strength or Vitality if used for partial melee [[MECHANICS/Combat/Components/Blocking\|blocking]].  
  
   - Purely arcane-based characters will appreciate the low stamina cost, ensuring more resources are funneled into spell usage and movement.

4. **Survival & Exploration**  
   
   - Some foci impart minor environment-based perks like conjurable lights, illusions to mislead beasts, or partial wards against curses—helpful for extended dungeon dives.  
  
   - Freed-up “hand” in a one-handed focus approach preserves your capacity to carry torches, pick locks, or manipulate levers mid-exploration.

## Use Cases

- **Pure Arcanist**: Equips a **two-handed staff** for large attribute bonuses in Intelligence, fueling destructive spells or illusions.  

- **Hybrid Ranger-Mage**: Wields a **bow in the main hand**, a **small orb** in the off-hand for modest INT boosts—allowing illusions or cantrip usage mid-combat.  

- **Summoner Specialist**: Relies on a **MIND-boosting grimoire**, possibly dual-wielding with a conjuration wand to maintain multiple summons or illusions simultaneously.

---

## Summary

Arcane Foci in *Chronicle* unify the **spellcasting experience**, bridging low-cost gear with potent attribute bonuses in either **Intelligence** or **Mind**, or both for the rare and legendary items. By tailoring whether you opt for a single off-hand orb or a massive two-handed staff, you shape your arcane identity: a swift conjurer weaving illusions, a powerful pyromancer scorching foes, or a cunning hybrid fighter dancing between weapon strikes and spell blasts.
