<center>THE CHANNEL OF FOCUS</center> 

---

Amulets occupy the **neck slot** in *Chronicle* and are typically **focused** items of magical or spiritual power. While [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Equipment/Accessories/Rings\|rings]] or cloaks often enhance survival or environmental adaptation, amulets emphasize **attribute bonuses** and **protective enchantments**, making them highly sought after among adventurers striving to surpass their natural limits or shield themselves from Velmoryth’s manifold threats.

---

## Core Attributes & Blanket Enchantments

- **Attribute Boosts:**  
  Many amulets grant **+1 to +3** (or higher, at advanced tiers) to key stats such as **[[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Attributes/Strength\|Strength]]**, **[[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Attributes/Intelligence\|Intelligence]]**, or **[[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Attributes/Mind\|Mind]]**. This direct attribute scaling can unlock threshold requirements for high-level gear or advanced spells.

- **Protection Effects:**  
  Some amulets provide **blanket defense** across multiple damage types—e.g., *Arcane Warding*, *Elemental Dampening*, or *Unholy Barrier*. Instead of focusing on raw attribute boosts, they offer broad safety nets in complex encounters.

> *Example:* A basic **Amulet of Minor Protection** might reduce all incoming elemental damage by 3–5%, while a more potent **Amulet of Ward** could block up to 10–15%, scaling with [[MECHANICS/Difficulty/Components/Item Level\|item level]].

---

## Rare Active Skill Amulets

Amulets are among the **few accessory types** that occasionally grant **active skills**. These unique items allow characters to **channel** a specialized effect once per cooldown or under specific conditions, e.g.:

- **Ward Shield**; Briefly conjures a protective barrier around the wearer, soaking a percentage of incoming damage or negating a single lethal strike.  

- **Soul Beacon**; Summons an ethereal ally or temporarily boosts [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Attributes/Faith\|Faith]]-based powers.  

- **Mirror Veil**; Reflects a fraction of spell damage back at attackers for a few seconds.

Such amulets are typically **rare or legendary** and demand higher attributes (Mind, Intelligence, or Faith) to unleash their abilities without backlash.

---

## Item Level & Slot Synergy

### Item Level (IL)

- As with all gear, amulets contribute to your total **Item Level**, influencing your overall attribute potential.  

- Low-IL amulets might only provide minor stat buffs, while **high-IL** amulets can combine multiple resistances or advanced features (like active skills).

### Slot Exclusivity

- Because **neck slot** items are limited to one, choosing the right amulet demands strategic consideration: do you prioritize raw attribute boosts, blanket protection, or a unique active skill?

---

## Interplay with Other Systems

1. **Combat & Defense**  
   
   - Protective or attribute-boosting amulets can transform a fragile caster into a robust ward-weaver, or grant a melee fighter enough Faith or Mind to dabble in minor invocations.

2. **Survival**  
   
   - Resistances from amulets reduce reliance on potions or specialized [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Equipment/Armor/Armor\|armor]]. This can be crucial in zones plagued by **Arcane storms** or **Fiery wastelands**.

3. **Advanced Crafting & Enchantment**  
   
   - Skilled enchanters may combine a mundane amulet’s base trait (e.g., +1 Intelligence) with a specialized magical effect. High-tier amulets might require **rare reagents** from boss creatures or hidden shrines.

---

## Summary

Amulets in *Chronicle* define a character’s **core enhancement**, focusing on attribute bumps or universal [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Skills and Talents/Physical Skills/Defensive/Defensive\|Defensive]] powers. While simpler variants provide modest attribute increases, **rare enchanted amulets** can revolutionize a build through potent protective wards or a single-use active skill. Whether you seek a straightforward stat boost, a broad damage shield, or an elegant game-changing [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Skills and Talents/Magic/Magical Skills/Invocation/Invocation\|Invocation]], your amulet slot remains a **pivotal** choice along your journey in Velmoryth.
