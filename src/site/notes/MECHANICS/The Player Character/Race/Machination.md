## Race Sheet 

Living suits of blazing-silver armor, powered by a crystalline **Soul Cage**.

They neither eat, drink, nor sleep, but must **burn soul-fuel** and undergo **periodic maintenance** to stay functional.  

Most city-states distrust them, iron memories of the Elvish Liberation die hard.

### Upkeep & Resource Economy

| Timer | Resources Required | On-Time Benefits | Stage-1 Overdue* | Stage-2 Overdue |
|-------|-------------------|------------------|------------------|-----------------|
| **Fuel Recycle** — **72 h** | 1 × *Automaton Oil* **or** crystal worth 25 MP | Full power, +10 % SP regen | **Low Power** (−50 % SP regen, −10 % atk spd) | **Shutdown** (no SP regen, −30 % atk spd) |
| **Maintenance** — **10 days** | *Gear Grease Kit* + Wrench + Cloth | Restore 10 % Max HP, clear “Rust” | **Wear-Down** (+20 % posture dmg taken, armor degrades +25 %) | **Stall** (move 50 %, dmg taken +50 %) |

\*Stage-1 penalties begin once the timer exceeds 150 % of its interval (Fuel ≥ 96 h, Maintenance ≥ 15 days).  
Stage-2 triggers beyond that.

**Inventory Footprint**

* *Automaton Oil*  - 1 Bulk, stack to 3 (covers nine days).  

* *Gear Grease Kit* - 2 Bulk, reusable five times.

### System Synergy

* **Survival** - food/water weight replaced by upkeep supplies; long treks demand oil planning rather than rations.  

* **[[MECHANICS/Survival/Components/Temperature\|Temperature]]** - immune to heat ≤ 60 °C; sub-zero temps thicken oil, doubling SP costs unless Emberwine is consumed.  

* **[[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Stealth\|Stealth]]** - perpetual gear-whirr adds +20 % footstep noise; sneaking relies on allies’ [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Skills and Talents/Magic/Magical Skills/Illusionism/Illusion\|Illusion]] or smoke bombs.  

* **[[MECHANICS/The Player Character/General Stats/Encumbrance\|Encumbrance]]** - STR 16 + Bulk bonus let Machinations haul camp gear or weapon racks for party.

### World-Level Balance

Enormous STR lets Machinations down elites fast, elevating **[[MECHANICS/Difficulty/Components/Character Level\|Character Level]]**, thus **[[MECHANICS/Difficulty/Components/World Level\|World Level]]**.  
The 72 h / 10-day upkeep cadence forces periodic retreats or cargo trains, throttling perpetual grind loops.

---

### Narrative Hooks

* **Legacy of Chains** - merchant surcharges, town entry bans; social quests to earn trust.  

* **Soul-Cage Retuning** - side-quest swaps +5 MIN for +3 DEX (less maintenance drain vs. modest dodge).  

* **Iron Revenant Hunt** - over-harvesting feral parts spawns region-wide boss event.

> Machinations are unyielding juggernauts, immune to spellfire, devastating in melee, but slaves to oil, grease, and the ticking heart of a stolen soul.
