## Race Sheet Drakes

Humanoid off‑spring of the great dragons, Drakes fuse mortal [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Skills and Talents/Physical Skills/Versatility/Versatility\|versatility]] with elemental might.  
Five known lineages exist—**Fire, Frost, Lightning, Acid,** and near‑mythic **Abyssal**—each inheriting a matching **breath weapon** and damage resistance.  
( Breath attacks are *innate* and **do not add to [[MECHANICS/Difficulty/Components/Character Level\|Character Level]]**.)

### System Synergy

* **[[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Skills and Talents/Magic/Magical Skills/Destruction/Destruction\|Destruction]] School** - Breath element counts as “same element” for *Imbue* and *Storm Cage* amplifiers (+10 % dmg).  

* **Stamina Management** - Wing‑glide offers vertical traversal at the cost of 15 Stamina; chaining glides can exhaust Drakes quickly on climbs.  

* **[[MECHANICS/Survival/Components/Temperature\|Temperature]] Loop** - Heat immunity frees pack weight in deserts; cold zones require Emberwine or Fur Cloak, tying into Preparedness.  

* **Intimidate Checks** - Draconic Presence adds +10 to social Attribute Tests; pairs well with [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Attributes/Strength\|Strength]] or [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Attributes/Faith\|Faith]] intimidate builds.

### World‑Level Implications

Drakes’ high base offensive stats can accelerate kill speed, risking **[[MECHANICS/Difficulty/Components/World Level\|World Level]]** escalation. Low Vitality means they remain vulnerable, encouraging precise, high‑burst combat rather than drawn‑out brawls.

---

### Role‑play & Narrative Hooks

* **Cult Enigma** - Unlock personal lore by finding Vorric cult ruins; each grants a *Lineage Requiem* quest improving resist aura.  

* **Dragon Tithe** - Certain towns demand a “heat tax”; Drakes may pay in Emberwine or perform smithy shifts, integrating crafting mini‑games.  

* **Abyssal Outcasts** - Abyssal Drakes trigger fear reactions in rural NPCs, affecting barter prices and quest doors.

Drakes stride the line between apex predator and fragile outcast, gliding over ambushes, spewing elemental death, yet forever chasing warmth, sustenance, and acceptance in a world that sees echoes of Hubris in their very scales.
