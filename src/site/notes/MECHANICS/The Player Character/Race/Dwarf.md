### Muscle & Stone 

* **[[MECHANICS/The Player Character/General Stats/Encumbrance\|Encumbrance]] Edge** - VIT 8 and +5 Bulk let Dwarves haul [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Equipment/Shields/Tower Shields\|tower shields]], spare weapons, or mining kits before hitting Burdened tier.  

* **Weapon Scaling** - STR 9 unlocks [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Skills and Talents/Physical Skills/Martial/Martial\|Martial]] skills (mace, greataxe) immediately; with *Ale‑forged*, peak STR hits 10–11, pushing early damage thresholds and [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Attributes/Attribute Checks\|Attribute Checks]] (e.g., breaking barred doors).  

* **Cold Expeditions** - Frost‑bitten peaks and Gloam Mire nights drain [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/General Stats/Stamina\|stamina]] and HP more slowly, freeing pack space otherwise spent on Emberwine or Fur Cloaks.

### Ale‑forged in the Mechanics Web

| Liquor Type   | Buff Window | Stout Effect                                     | Additional Note                    |
| ------------- | ----------- | ------------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------- |
| Common Ale    | 90 s        | -15 % posture dmg, +20 % STR                     | No Reflex penalty for Dwarves.     |
| Dwarven Stout | 120 s       | -20 % posture dmg, +25 % STR                     | Adds +5 HP regen /min for Dwarves. |
| Emberwine     | 90 s        | 10 % fire resist (all races) + Stout for Dwarves | Warmth stacks with cold resist.    |

*Humans may replicate *Stout* via the **Lush** talent but still suffer Reflex‑blur; no extra HP regen.*

**Strategic Uses**

1. **Boss Prep** - Chug Stout before shield‑walling a troll; higher STR improves stagger chance.  

2. **Smithing Marathon** - Liquor lowers posture loss while hammering, letting Dwarves craft longer before fatigue.  

3. **Stamina Counter** - While drunk, Stamina costs rise 10 %; Dwarves offset with STR damage spike and posture soak.

### Faith & Rune Work

* **Mountain Faithful** synergy with **[[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Skills and Talents/Magic/Magical Skills/Invocation/Invocation\|Invocation]]** school: +5 % baseline Holy damage/resist dovetails with FTH 7, allowing early access to Tier I heals and buffs.  

* **Rune Etching** ([[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Skills and Talents/Magic/Magical Skills/Enchanting/Enchanting\|Enchanting]]) requires STR checks to chisel glyph plates; Dwarves pass automatically, solidifying their niche in party crafting.

### World‑Level & Narrative Tie‑ins

* Dwarven HP bonus slows early deaths but their bigger meals accelerate **[[MECHANICS/Difficulty/Components/Character Level\|Character Level]]** via frequent camp crafting. Plan World‑Level spikes carefully.  

* Lore hooks: scattered **Mother Clan relic caches** require high STR to unseal, natural Dwarf side quests that feed the exploration loop.

Dwarves thrive where others freeze or break. Sturdy frames, bottomless mugs, and the Mountain King’s blessing forge them into walking bulwarks—just keep the rations flowing and the [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Equipment/Armor/Armor\|armor]] cool when the sun climbs.
