The War of Hubris blasted the ley‑lines, leaving most advanced sorcery **“scarred.”**  
Low circles (Tier I-II) still channel pure [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/General Stats/Mana\|mana]]; higher circles (Tier III–IV) demand **sacrificial catalysts**—blood‑shards, siphoned life, or bound souls.  

Mis‑casting scarred spells risks back‑lash (HP loss, [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Status Effects/Madness\|Madness]]) but their power eclipses anything known in the last century.

| Circle | Fuel Only | Fuel + Sacrifice | Typical Catalyst          | Mis‑Cast Risk |
|--------|-----------|------------------|---------------------------|---------------|
| Tier I | ✓         | —                | —                         | None          |
| Tier II| ✓         | —                | Focus Dust / Glyph Ink    | 5 %           |
| Tier III| —        | ✓                | 1 Blood‑Shard **or** 25 HP| 12 %          |
| Tier IV | —        | ✓                | 3 Blood‑Shards **or** 75 HP| 20 %          |

## Destruction

[[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Skills and Talents/Magic/Magical Skills/Destruction/Destruction\|Destruction]] channels elemental wrath. Each element drains a different enemy resource- Frost erodes [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/General Stats/Stamina\|Stamina]], Lightning burns Mana, Fire sears [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/General Stats/Health\|Health]], and pure Arcane slows movement. Scarred tiers amplify scope and devastation at lethal cost.

| Element | Pure Spell (Tier I–II) | Mana | Effect                         | Scarred Spell (Tier IV) | Catalyst |
|---------|------------------------|------|--------------------------------|-------------------------|----------|
| Fire    | **Fireball**           | 18 MP| –40 HP, Ignite 5 s             | **Hellstorm** – 6 m cone, 200 HP over 4 s | 75 HP |
| Frost   | **Frost Spike**        | 16 MP| –30 HP, –25 Stamina            | **Absolute Zero** – Freezes AoE, steals 60 Stamina | 1 Shard |
| Lightning| **Chain Spark**       | 20 MP| –35 HP, –20 MP                 | **Storm Cage** – Chains to 5 foes, –50 MP each | 3 Shards |
| Arcane  | **Arcane Bolt**        | 15 MP| –32 HP, Slow 2 s               | **Void Lance** – Armor‑ignoring beam, heavy Slow | 75 HP |

## [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Skills and Talents/Magic/Magical Skills/Enchanting/Enchanting\|Enchanting]] 

Enchanters weave subtle magic: Sleep, [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Status Effects/Blindness\|Blindness]], or **Imbue Fire** (+25 Fire dmg for 60 s). At scarred ranks they rewrite energy signatures. **Infernal Brand** doubles damage but corrodes gear, while **Soul‑Brand** enslaves an item to the caster’s blood.

| Spell (Pure)    | Tier | Effect                                      | Scarred Upgrade (Tier IV)                 | Catalyst |
|-----------------|------|---------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------|----------|
| Sleep           | I    | Target slumbers 6 s                         | **Mass Lull** – 4 m AoE sleep             | 25 HP    |
| Imbue Fire      | II   | +25 Fire dmg / 60 s                         | **Infernal Brand** – +50 Fire, –10 % durability | 3 Shards|
| Blindness       | II   | Blind for 8 s                               | **Soul‑Brand** – +25 % dmg, drains wielder 1 HP/s | 3 Shards|

Conjurers fold space and raise the dead. Pure spells include **Portal (Mark/Jump)** and **Raise Skeleton**. Scarred adepts open can command a **Grave Legion**, five [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Race/Undead\|undead]] striking in unison, fed by the summoner’s blood.

| Spell | Pure Tier | Effect                                | Scarred Form (Tier)      | Catalyst |
|-------|-----------|---------------------------------------|--------------------------|----------|

| Raise Skeleton     | II | 1 skeleton ally 45 s            | **Grave Legion** (IV) – 5 undead, drains 10 HP/min | 75 HP |
| Control Undead     | III| Command undead 30 s             | **Bind Wight** (III) – permanent thrall | 1 Shard |

Illusionists twist perception: **Throw Voice** lures patrols; **Illusory Companion** draws fire for ten seconds. In scarred rites, sonic horrors like **Dread Chime** panic mobs, and **Nightmare Deluge** bathes minds in blind [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Status Effects/Terror\|terror]].

## Transfiguration 

Transfigurers reshape substance. **Stoneskin** hardens flesh; **Metal Shift** turns iron to silver. Their scarred pinnacle petrifies living beings with **Flesh → Stone** or animates statues at the cost of the caster’s own life‑force.

| Spell                  | Pure Tier | Effect                               | Scarred Upgrade                | Catalyst |
|------------------------|-----------|--------------------------------------|--------------------------------|----------|
| Stoneskin              | II        | +40 % phys resist, –10 % speed 30 s  | —                              | —        |
| Metal Shift            | III       | Iron → Silver weapon 5 min           | Brittleness on fail            | 1 Shard |
| Animate Object         | III       | Control statue/armor 60 s            | Costs 25 HP per extra minute   | 25 HP |
| Flesh → Stone          | IV        | Permanent petrify                    | Limb petrify on mis‑cast       | 75 HP |

## Psionic 

Psionicists wield internal power—immune to shard mechanics but vulnerable to **Mind Overload** (stun if > 75 MP burned in 10 s). Telekinetic shoves reposition foes; **Mental Projection** scouts ahead; **Cerebral Rupture** detonates neural shockwaves that shred [[MECHANICS/Combat/Components/Posture\|posture]] and will.

| Spell             | Tier | MP Cost | Effect                              |
|-------------------|------|---------|-------------------------------------|
| Telekinetic Pull  | I    | 8       | Drag target 4 m                     |
| Telekinetic Push  | I    | 8       | Knockback 4 m                       |
| Mental Projection | II   | 18      | Free‑fly vision 15 s; body inert    |
| Psychic Wall      | III  | 35      | 50 % damage shield; MP drains on hits |
| Cerebral Rupture  | IV   | 60      | AoE brain‑shock; heavy HP & posture |

## Summary

* **Pure tiers** remain the province of cautious scholars—useful, reliable, never spectacular.  
* **Scarred tiers** unleash pre‑Hubris potency, but every shard spent or drop of blood spilled leaves a mark—Arcane Corrosion (‑5 % Max HP, ‑10 MP) or Madness if overused.  
* **Build Identity** thrives: fire‑slinging blood‑mages, [[MECHANICS/The Player Character/Skills and Talents/Magic/Magical Skills/Illusionism/Illusion\|illusion]] tricksters, necromantic generals, psionic scouts—each with clear strengths and well‑telegraphed risks.

In Velmoryth, mastery lies not in how loudly you cast—but in how cleverly you decide *what you’re willing to give up* for power.
