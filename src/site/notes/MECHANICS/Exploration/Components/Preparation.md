In *Chronicle*, **Preparedness** stands at the crossroads of **[[MECHANICS/Exploration/Exploration Overview\|exploration]]** and **[[MECHANICS/Survival/Survival Overview\|survival]]**. Every journey no matter how short demands careful thought about what you bring, how you’ll manage basic needs like [[MECHANICS/Survival/Components/Hunger\|Hunger]] and [[MECHANICS/Survival/Components/Thirst\|Thirst]], and whether you’re physically capable of enduring the rigors of uncharted terrain. Lack of preparation can lead to [[MECHANICS/Survival/Components/Disease\|Disease]], [[MECHANICS/Survival/Components/Fatigue\|Fatigue]], or [[MECHANICS/Survival/Components/Injuries\|Injuries]], forcing you to learn quickly that **Velmoryth punishes the careless.**

---

## 1. Why Preparedness Matters

1. **Hunger & Thirst**  
   
   - Adequate **food and water** are essential, as hunger and dehydration quickly erode stamina, increasing fatigue and vulnerability in combat.  Stockpiling rations for extended treks- while balancing pack weight, can save you from starvation or the need to forage desperately in dangerous areas.

1. **Fatigue & [[MECHANICS/Survival/Components/Temperature\|Temperature]]**  
   
   - Traveling across **frozen slopes**, scorching deserts, or swampy bogs saps energy. Having proper clothing, shelter, and the means to make a fire helps stave off hypothermia or heat exhaustion. Regular rest periods aren’t luxuries; they’re vital for stamina recovery and mental clarity, without them, your [[MECHANICS/Exploration/Components/Mapping\|Mapping]] and [[MECHANICS/Exploration/Components/Navigation\|Navigation]] suffer.

3. **Disease Prevention & Injury Recovery**  
   
   - **Bandages**, **antitoxins**, and **basic medicines** keep illnesses at bay if you traverse plague-ridden marshes or come in contact with infected creatures.  Proper healing measures, like splints or ointments, prevent minor injuries from escalating into crippling wounds when far from civilization.

3. **[[MECHANICS/The Player Character/General Stats/Encumbrance\|Encumbrance]]**  
   
   - Overloading your pack with supplies slows your pace, raises stamina consumption, and hampers mobility in fights. Under-packing risks running out of necessities. Striking a careful balance means you’ll handle threats and survive the environment without collapsing under the weight of your gear.

---

## The Role of Preparedness in Exploration

1. **Mapping & Planning**  
   
   - Detailed **maps** and prior knowledge of your route inform how many supplies to carry. A shorter path may need fewer rations, but might demand climbing gear or cold-weather gear if it crosses higher elevations.  If your notes warn of disease outbreaks in The Gloam Mire, you’ll pack extra medical supplies, reinforcing the synergy between mapping and prepping. Of course you can purchase a map to better prepare yourself, but in some cases you may be *going in blind*.

2. **Navigation & Adaptation**  
   
   - The wilds hold surprises: unexpected weather shifts, hidden ravines, or hostile creatures can derail even well-planned journeys. Carrying additional **rope**, a **light source**, or **protective gear** can transform a potential dead end into a mere detour.

3. **Survival-Earned Discovery**  
   
   - Because *Chronicle* demands rigorous management of thirst, temperature, and fatigue, truly *reaching* a remote crypt or mountain shrine is an accomplishment in itself.  Progress isn’t measured in quest completions alone, it’s the journey, where your forethought and gear selection keep you alive long enough to unearth hidden wonders.

---

## Strategies for Effective Preparedness

1. **Categorize Your Needs**  
   
   - Food, water, basic first aid. Insulation for cold zones, insect repellent or antidotes for swamps, protective masks for ashen wastes.  Extra torches, reserve bandages, or potions- kept light but sufficient to handle a few surprises.

2. **Account for Encumbrance**  
   
   - Divvy up weight if traveling in a group. Each party member can specialize in carrying certain resources. Solo travelers often compromise by taking fewer weapons or luxuries to ensure they can haul crucial survival gear.

3. **Plan Rest & Recovery**  
   
   - Outline potential **campsites** or safe shelters along your route, these are invaluable for tending to disease, mending injuries, and updating your map with newly gained knowledge.  Ignoring rest leads to faster fatigue buildup, making you more susceptible to disease and injury, and less effective in [[MECHANICS/Combat/Combat Overview\|combat]].

---

## Conclusion

Preparedness in *Chronicle* is the **bedrock** upon which all exploration stands. Meticulously balancing your supplies and evaluating potential dangers before setting out determines whether your excursion ends in triumph or tragedy. By weaving together **mapping, hunger, thirst, fatigue, temperature management, disease prevention, injury recovery, and encumbrance** into a single interconnected system, *Chronicle* ensures that every step across Velmoryth’s wild expanse is earned. A well-planned expedition can pierce even the darkest of places, while one marred by carelessness stands little chance against the world’s relentless challenges.
