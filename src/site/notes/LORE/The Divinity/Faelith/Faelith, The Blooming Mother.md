<center>THE GODDESS OF LIFE, GROWTH, AND REBIRTH</center>

----
![FAELITH_PORTRAIT.jpg](/img/user/IMAGES/FAELITH_PORTRAIT.jpg)

Faelith was the weaver of life, the endless cycle of renewal that sustained Velmoryth.

Her touch brought the forests to bloom, rivers to flow, and beasts to roam free. She is depicted as a towering figure of vines, blossoms, and shifting petals, with eyes that glow like deep pools of verdant energy.
