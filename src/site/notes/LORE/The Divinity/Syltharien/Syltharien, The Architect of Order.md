<center>THE GOD OF LOGIC</center>

---
![SYLTHARIEN_PORTRAIT.jpg](/img/user/IMAGES/SYLTHARIEN_PORTRAIT.jpg)

Cold, calculating, and inexorable, Syltharien sought to impose perfect order upon all things. He built the [[LORE/Pieces of Power/The Eternal Lexicon\| Eternal Lexicon]], the laws that governed magic and reality, and created the Elves to keep his divine vision alive. He is depicted as a figure of silver and marble, adorned with ever-brilliant runes that shift upon his form.

When the [[LORE/Mortals/Elves\|Elves]] rebelled and shattered the Lexicon, he waged war upon them, leading to the unraveling of the worldâ€™s balance and the fraying of the fabric of reality. As his last line of defense, he created [[LORE/Mortals/Machinations\|Machinations]] and subsequently the [[LORE/Mortals/Machinations\| Soul Cages.]] 

His [[LORE/The Land/Landmarks/Impossible Tombs\|Impossible Tombs]] - once his grand libraries, are now paradox-ridden and unfathomable to the common person. Reality folds in on itself here, it is unadulterated chaos. Without the [[LORE/The Divinity/Syltharien/Syltharien, The Architect of Order\| Architect]], there can be no grand design.
