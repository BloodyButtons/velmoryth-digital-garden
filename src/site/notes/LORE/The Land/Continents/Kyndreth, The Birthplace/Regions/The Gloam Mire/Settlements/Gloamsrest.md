<center>A VILLAGE HIDDEN IN THE MIST </center>

---

![GLOAMSREST_PORTRAIT.jpg](/img/user/IMAGES/GLOAMSREST_PORTRAIT.jpg)

---

Nestled in the very heart of **[[LORE/The Land/Continents/Kyndreth, The Birthplace/Regions/The Gloam Mire/The Gloam Mire\|The Gloam Mire]]**, where the suffocating mist dares not creep and the trees no longer whisper secrets, lies **Gloamsrest**, a hidden sanctuary for the wary and the lost. It is a village half-swallowed by the land itself, its homes carved into **ancient roots, moss-laden ruins, and hollowed-out trees**, each dwelling blending seamlessly with the Mireâ€™s eerie embrace. **Pale lanterns glow softly in the perpetual night**, their light never seeming to reach too far, as if reluctant to offend the surrounding darkness.

Gloamsrest is a place of **quiet survival**, where people speak in **hushed voices**, wary of waking whatever listens in the Mire. It is one of the last places where the land does not **actively consume its inhabitants** - a fragile haven on the precipice of oblivion. The villagers **barter in whispers**, exchanging tinctures, dried herbs, and relics scavenged from the depths of the swamp. **Coin has little meaning here**, for the economy is one of necessity rather than greed. What is needed is given, what is taken is returned in kind.

**Superstitions dictate daily life.** No one ventures beyond the treeline without a **marked talisman** carved from the bone of some long-forgotten beast. At sundown, doors are shut and sealed with thick vines **not for fear of bandits or beasts, but of the Mire itself, which sometimes shifts and reshapes paths in the dead of night.** The villagers **never call each other by name after dark**, lest something else remember it in the morning.

It is guarded by [[LORE/Factions/The Gloamwardens\|The Gloamwardens]] faithfully. Day and night they patrol the streets and watch the outskirts for any sign of impending danger. For them, although Gloamsrest is not safe, it is the closest thing left to home.
